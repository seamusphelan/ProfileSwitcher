Usage

NB - this will only work on phones that have ProfileManager (e.g. CyanogenMod builds)

Create schedules for when you want your phone to automatically change profiles. To create a schedule, chose "New schedule" in the menu. Choose the profile you want to switch, the time of day (24 hour clock) and which days of the week.
For example: Imagine you have profiles Work, Home and Night. You could have the following schedules.

  • Work @ 08:00 on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday & Friday
  • Home @ 10:30 on Saturday & Sunday
  • Home @ 17:30 on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday & Friday
  • Night @ 23:30 on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday & Sunday

You can also set timed profiles - similar to the old Nokias :-) for once off occasions. e.g Going to the cinema. Choose "Timed Profile", pick profile Silent for 2 hours.

Headset plug/unplug reset option.
Standard behaviour in Android is that plugging in a headset causes the volume settings etc. to switch to what they were the last time a headset was plugged in. Likewise for unplugging a headset. If you change a profile (either manually or using this app) and then plug/unplug a headset, the volume settings will be changed (but the active profile will remain the same)
This is reason you may want to have the profile reset after a headset is plugged/unplugged.

Timed profile.
At the end of a timed profile, it will revert to the profile that was active before the timed profile was created. If a scheduled profile change was supposed to happen, before the time profile has finished, it will revert to this profile.
